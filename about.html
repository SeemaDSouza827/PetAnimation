About US

Pet Shelter is a brand of Merry Tails Pet Solutions Pvt Ltd. - a pet care company dedicated to creating services and solutions keeping the dog at the center of everything. We put dogs first and that reflects in every decision we take w.r.t products and services that we create and operate. The company has been set-up by - Sarvmeet Oberoi ( pet parent of 3 lovely dogs over a span of 18 yrs ) , who after working long stints in large corporates , decided to set up Petfelix to try and fill up the huge gap existing in India in quality pet care services. The boarding services are a starting point for the company which aims to expand to other aspects of pet care in the coming years.
